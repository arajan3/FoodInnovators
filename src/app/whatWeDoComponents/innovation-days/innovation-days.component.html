The Sydney & Melbourne Restaurant scene is so dynamic and vibrant that it’s difficult to keep track of the latest & most exciting chefs and their menus &  we often look to restaurant menus as an early indicator of key food trends. Recognizing that it’s important for our wider team to be across what’s new & innovative in the market  twice a year we curate the best of Sydney & Melbourne chefs and bring them into our food theatre for the day to demonstrate a few dishes from their menus to taste and to talk emerging food trends, ingredients & more.
